Welcome to the Court Journalists Association of Kenya

"Our mission is to enhance the quality of legal reporting by empowering journalists with specialized knowledge and resources. We are dedicated to promoting excellence in court journalism through continuous training and support." - CJAK

The Court Journalists Association of Kenya (CJAK) provides a comprehensive array of resources and support to journalists covering legal and court proceedings. Our objective is to foster professionalism and improve the quality of court reporting across Kenya.

Over 15 Years of Expertise

Since our founding, the Court Journalists Association of Kenya has been dedicated to advancing the field of court journalism through specialized training, research, and advocacy. We have continuously worked to enhance the skills of court journalists, promote best practices, and support professional development across the legal and media sectors in Kenya and beyond.

Tailored Solutions for Court Journalists

We understand that each court journalist's needs are unique. Our approach is tailored to address the specific requirements of our members, offering customized training programs, resources, and support. Our goal is to enhance the capabilities of individual journalists and improve the overall standards of court reporting in Kenya, ensuring that our members stay at the forefront of their profession.
